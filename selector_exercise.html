The "content" paragraph bundle is perhaps the most simple bundle we can think of. It just provides a single WYSIWYG textarea. What is the use of that? It gives content creators the ability to easily add blocks into the sidebar or body of a page. If your WYSIWYG supports layout plugins, it is possible for editors to easily add in their own layouts so that they can enjoy the benefits of the grid provided by the base theme. The layouts you see in the WYSIWYG are not shipped with Paragraphs Pack, it is there just to give you an idea of some of the flexibility which the Content bundle can provide.
